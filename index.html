02_Why Moringa?

Interesting story, for the longest time, I had no idea what to do with my life. When I heard about software development, I knew I had to be part of the industry. This is the only industry where I get to be creative, to be full wave nerdy, and to change people's lives using my laptop. After asking around, everyone recommended Moringa School. I am here because I believe that this institution has the capacity to make me a world-class professional. I also get to be a woman in technology and inspire other women to join the tech industry. How cool is that!
